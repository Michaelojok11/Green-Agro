About Us

The company is a privatley owned company under the management of 2 partners, launched and incorporated in 2022. Green Agro is a company that was started founded in 2022.Green Agribusiness is a company that deals in agriculture by using smart technology to support agriculture in a sustainable way. The company works towards finding solutions to maximize the economic, environmental and social sustainability of agriculture and agroindustry. It is well established company that specialises in providing smart and sustainable agricultural solutions. The company headoffice is located in Stockholm Sweden. Our company is built on trust, competence, trust and above all innovation. Our services ranges from; Trainings on the use of Agricultural machines, smart agriculture, sustainable farming, search for markets for agricultural products, Fertilizers, Trainings on the use of agricultural machineries
